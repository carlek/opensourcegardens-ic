Werde auch du eine Open Source Gärtner*in!

You too become an open-source gardener!

Whether it's in a community garden, in your own backyard, on your balcony or in your room window, joining the movement is easy! Start planting open source seeds, then tell us and others about it and connect.

To make it as easy as possible for you to get started, we started the campaign "Connecting Gardens" in 2021 . In this campaign we sent out different types of collected open source seeds with basic recommendations for cultivation. We would like to start a similar campaign again in spring 2022. Join in! Or order the seeds directly from the breeders. And if you don't have a garden or even if gardening isn't really your thing but you think the idea is good: You can always take part online and get involved. For example in our matrix room: https://matrix.to/#/#open-source-gardens : matrix.org

Read about other ways to get involved on the homepage of Open Source Gardens .
